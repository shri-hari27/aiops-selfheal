Thank you for installing AIOps Self-Healing Demo.

Useful commands:

Check Release

helm list

Check Pods

kubectl get pods -n {{ .Values.namespace.name }}

Check Service

kubectl get svc -n {{ .Values.namespace.name }}

Upgrade

helm upgrade aiops .

Rollback

helm rollback aiops 1

History

helm history aiops

Uninstall

helm uninstall aiops
